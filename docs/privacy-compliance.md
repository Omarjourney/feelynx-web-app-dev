# Privacy and Compliance

## Data Retention Schedule

- User posts and messages are removed after the retention period defined in each user's privacy settings (default 30 days).
- Payment records are retained for financial auditing for 90 days.

## Anonymized Analytics

- Analytics events are stripped of personal identifiers before storage.
- User identifiers are replaced with hashed values to prevent tracking.

## GDPR and CCPA Requests

- Users can download or delete their data via the privacy settings page or `/privacy/:userId` API.
- Requests are processed within 30 days. Deleted data is irrecoverable.
- Contact support for additional privacy inquiries.
